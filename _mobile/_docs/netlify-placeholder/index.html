How it works

From idea to product in four stages

Each conversation builds on the last. The agent challenges your thinking, produces structured documents, and tracks your progress across sessions. Work in 20-minute bursts — on the bus, at night, during lunch.

1

Share your idea

Open your messaging app and type "mentor." Describe your idea in plain language — no jargon, no templates, no forms.

2

Structure your thinking

The Mentor walks you through Working Backwards, Lean Canvas, competitive analysis, and more. Each framework produces a real document.

3

Validate and refine

Test assumptions, map your market, pressure-test unit economics, and define your brand. The same system that asks hard questions helps you answer them.

4

Build and deploy

The Mentor transitions from strategy to execution — prototype, design, and deploy. Your product goes live without you writing a line of code.
