## 9. Revision Checklist

_Testable questions for every draft. Yes/No — no ambiguity._

- [ ] Does this sound like a real person wrote it?
- [ ] Would I say this out loud to someone I respect?
- [ ] Does every paragraph earn its place — could any be cut without loss?
- [ ] Are emotional moments shown, not told?
- [ ] Is the opening friction clear by paragraph 1?
- [ ] Does the ending add something new — not just restate?
