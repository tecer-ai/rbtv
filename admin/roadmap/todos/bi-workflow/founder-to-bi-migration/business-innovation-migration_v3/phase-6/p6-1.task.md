## BMAD Integration Philosophy

The goal is NOT to replicate BMAD capabilities. Instead:
- **Route to BMAD** when BMAD has equivalent/superior functionality
- **Create RBTV workflow** only for founder-specific frameworks with no BMAD equivalent
- **Bridge workflows** that prepare context for BMAD workflows
