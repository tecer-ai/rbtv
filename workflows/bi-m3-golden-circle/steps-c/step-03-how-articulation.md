Define 2-3 How principles — the unique approach, values, or process through which the brand delivers on its Why.

## MANDATORY SEQUENCE

### 1. Connect Why to Action

Start with the Why:

> "Your Why is: '[Why statement]'
>
> Now we ask: If we believe this, how does that belief change the way we build, sell, and support our product compared to alternatives?"

### 2. Extract How Signals

From prior frameworks:

**From Brand Prism Personality:**
- Traits that describe how the brand acts
- Behavioral examples from each trait

**From Brand Prism Relationship:**
- Relationship type and style
- How the brand relates to customers

**From Lean Canvas Unfair Advantage:**
- Structural or capability differentiators
- What's hard to copy

**From Brand Archetypes:**
- Behavioral traits beyond voice
- Action patterns

Present: "Here are the signals that inform your How..."

### 3. Draft How Principles

Guide founder to articulate 2-3 How principles:

**Format:** "We [action/approach] because we believe [connection to Why]."

**Examples:**
- "We show our reasoning, not just conclusions, because we believe understanding creates trust."
- "We challenge before we comfort because we believe growth requires honest feedback."
- "We design for clarity over features because we believe simplicity enables confidence."

For each principle, verify:

> "Can a customer or team member SEE this in action? Where would they observe it?"

### 4. Test Observable

For each How principle:

> "Give me a concrete example of this How in your product, communication, or support."

If no example exists or could exist → principle is aspirational, not operational

Revise until each How has observable manifestation.

### 5. Test Differentiating

For each How principle:

> "Do your competitors operate this way? Or do they prioritize differently?"

If competitors do the same → not differentiating

Ask:
> "What would a competitor have to change about their product, process, or culture to copy this How?"

Revise until each How is distinct.

### 6. Test Connection to Why

For each How principle:

> "If I removed your Why statement, would this How still make sense? Or does it lose its rationale?"

If the How makes sense without the Why → connection is weak

Revise until the Why-How connection is explicit and necessary.

### 7. Test Unfair Advantage Reflection

Check that at least one How reflects Lean Canvas Unfair Advantage:

> "Your Unfair Advantage is [statement]. Which How principle reflects this?"

If none → add a How that captures the structural differentiator

### 8. Order by Importance

Rank the How principles:

> "Which How is most distinctive and visible? That goes first."

Order: Most distinctive → supporting principles

### 9. Update golden-circle.md

Update the How section with:
- 2-3 How principles in priority order
- Observable examples for each
- Connection to Why for each
